# Literature

- Try reading at least 40 to 50 papers to expand your knowledge on the topic.
- If you are only able to find one research paper on a certain aspect/topic, then look at the references in those paper to find.
- Try going for journals from good publications.

# Presentation

- Start with the objectives, explain the title and stress on its relevance.
- Don’t focus on methodology and conclusions too much (in the beginning of your project), you are just exploring and are open to inputs at this point.
- Present your key takeaways only. People can read your slides.
