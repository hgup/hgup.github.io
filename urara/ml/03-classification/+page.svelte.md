### Precision & Recall

- **Precision** refers to "How many times were you right about a Positive?"
  $$
\text{precision} = \frac{TP}{TP + FP}
$$
- **Recall** refers to "How many positives could you recall correctly?"
  $$
\text{recall} = \frac{TP}{TP + FN}
$$
